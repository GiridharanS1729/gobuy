1. Introduction

Welcome to GoBuy ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our e-commerce platform.

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

Your payment information is processed through secure payment processors and is never stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
